Privacy Policy

Weather Dial does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

Data Sources

The app retrieves weather data from trusted third-party APIs such as Apple Weather, Google, AccuWeather, Microsoft, and the National Weather Service. We do not track or store your usage or location.

Third-Party Services

Weather Dial may link to external services to display weather data. Please refer to those providers for their privacy policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@weatherdial.app.
