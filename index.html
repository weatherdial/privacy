Privacy Policy

Last updated:July 28, 2025

At Weather Dial, your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your data. In short: we don’t collect any personal information—period.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • We do not collect your name, email, location, or IP address.
  • We do not use any analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or App Tracking).
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking technologies.
  • We do not sell or share any user information—because we don’t collect any.

Weather Data

Weather Dial displays forecast information by retrieving data from trusted third-party weather providers, including:

  • Apple Weather
  • Google
  • AccuWeather
  • Microsoft
  • National Weather Service (NWS)

These providers may have their own privacy practices when delivering weather data. We do not control or transmit any personal information to them.

Children’s Privacy

Weather Dial does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for all ages and complies with applicable children’s privacy regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change the way we handle your data, we will update this policy and notify you directly within the app before any new practices go into effect.
